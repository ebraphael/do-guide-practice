A Letter from the Editor

In the Paint

Nicholas Alumkal, Sports Editor

Dear Readers,

Syracuse lacrosse stands at a crossroads. After a season that tested the program's resilience, the Orange enter 2026 with renewed purpose and determination. This special edition examines the players, the strategies, and the stories that will define this pivotal season.

Our team of dedicated student journalists has spent months covering every aspect of Syracuse lacrosse—from the recruiting trails to the practice fields, from the coaching staff's vision to the players' personal journeys. What emerges is a portrait of a program refusing to be defined by its setbacks.

As you read these stories, you'll discover the individuals who embody Syracuse lacrosse's fighting spirit. These are young athletes who understand the weight of the Orange jersey and the expectations that come with it. They're ready to write their own chapter in this storied program's history.

Thank you for reading.

— Nicholas Alumkal
